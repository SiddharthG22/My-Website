Leadership

Siddharth Ganapathy

Founder and CEO

Siddharth was once a high schooler who has struggled with carrying his backpack. Doing this, every single day started to impact other aspects of his life, including his golf game. And so, in early January of 2020, he founded this company on the principle that no high schooler should suffer any injuries, as a result of carrying backpacks. Through this service, Siddharth wants high schoolers to enjoy school to the fullest, without the annoyance of any physical problem that stems from carrying backpacks. Pricing

Obviously, this service isn't free. There are several payment methods. Adhering to company policy, our backpack couriers can only walk for five miles. However, depending on the backpack courier, they may agree to walk a legal meximum of three more miles, or not. For every extra mile walked, you must include a 20% tip, in addition to the final price. This is non-negotiable. Also, you can view payment methods below:

Mileage Cost (USD)
1 mile $15.00
2 miles $25.00
3 miles $35.00
4 miles $45.00
5 miles $55.00
+1 mile $55.00 + 20%
+2 miles $55.00 + 40%
+3 miles $55.00 + 60%

You can view the payment options below, and choose accordingly. If you decide to pay with cash, you will need to give it to the backpack courier up front. If you would like to send a paycheck, please do so, within two days after your courier service. Mail it to "The Backpack Couriers" for a given amount.
